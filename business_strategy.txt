рҹ”§ The 4 Roles (The Machine)
Role 1: рҹ§  Lead Teacher (YOU)
Position: Front of the class / moves between groups
What you do:

Teach new theory to small groups (5-8 students who are at the same level)
Handle HARD doubts that assistants can't solve
Decide each student's curriculum path (the app helps with this)
Quality check вҖ” make sure assistants are marking correctly
Why only you: This needs deep math knowledge + curriculum judgment

Role 2: вң… Correction Officer (Undergraduate, FIXED at a desk)
Position: Sits at a "Correction Desk" вҖ” students come to this desk
What they do:

Students finish an exercise вҶ’ bring their book to this desk
Checks answers against the answer key (you prepare these in advance)
Marks вң“ or вң— on each question
Enters results into the app (tap correct/wrong on the phone вҖ” your app makes this easy!)
Says "go back and fix Q3, Q7" вҖ” student goes back to their seat
Why an undergraduate can do this:

They just compare answers to an answer key вҖ” no teaching skill needed
Your app's score entry page is designed for exactly this (tap grid of вң“/вң—)
Any math undergraduate can verify answers
Pay: LKR 200-300/hour (part-time university student)

Role 3: рҹҡ¶ Floor Monitor (Undergraduate, WALKS around)
Position: Walks between student desks
What they do:

Helps with SIMPLE doubts ("Which formula for area of triangle?", "How do I simplify this fraction?")
Keeps students focused (prevents chatting, sleeping, phone use)
If a student is truly stuck вҶ’ raises hand вҶ’ YOU come to that student
Distributes exercise sheets/books
Manages the "queue" вҖ” tells students "take your book to the correction desk"
Why an undergraduate can do this:

Simple doubts only need Grade 11 level math knowledge
Any university math/engineering student knows this
They're not teaching theory вҖ” just giving small hints
Pay: LKR 200-300/hour (part-time university student)

Role 4: рҹ“ұ App Operator (Can be combined with Correction Officer)
Position: At the correction desk or a separate phone
What they do:

Enter all scores into the app as students get corrected
Generate and share leaderboard images to WhatsApp at end of class
Update student progress positions
Handle attendance marking
TIP

In the beginning, combine this with the Correction Officer role. One person corrects AND enters scores. Your app is phone-optimized, so this is fast вҖ” they correct the book, then immediately tap results into the app.
